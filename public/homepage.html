  • Named after Mars, the Roman God of War
  • Also referred to as the Red Planet due to the effects of iron oxide on the planet's surface
  • Mars and its red coloring can be seen from Earth with the naked eye
  • 4th planet from the Sun
  • 2nd smallest planet in our Solar System
  • 2 moons: Phobos and Deimos
  • Mars has days and seasons comparable to Earth
  • Orbital period is 687 (Earth) days
  • Mars has the largest volcano on any planet in our Solar System
  • Mars has the highest known mountain on any planet in our Solar System
  • Mars exploration:
    • Mariner 4 (unmanned spacecraft launched by NASA on 11/28/64)
    • Viking 1 (first successful landing on surface on 7/4/76)
    • Soviet Mars 3 (soft landing only in December 1971)
    • 7/4/97: Mars Pathfinder spacecraft
    • 7/5/97: Pathfinder launches Sojourner rover
  • 1/2004-6/2018: Mars Exploration Rovers Spirit and Opportunity
  • 12/23/2003: Mars Express Orbiter (European Space Agency)
  • 9/24/2014: Mars Orbiter Mission spacecraft (Indian Space Research Organization
